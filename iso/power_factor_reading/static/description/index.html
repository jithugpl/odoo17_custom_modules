Quick Guide: Power Factor Reading Module

The Power Factor Reading module allows you to effectively monitor and manage power factor readings. Access the form via the Odoo dashboard, enter the required details such as date, power factor value, and the time of reading. Verify the data for accuracy before saving. Regularly review all settings to ensure the module's efficiency. Contact support for any technical assistance.
