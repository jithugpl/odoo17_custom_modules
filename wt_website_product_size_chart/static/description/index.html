Website Product Size Chart

The Website Product Size Chart is designed to enhance the product listing pages on your Odoo website by displaying size charts for products. It allows customers to easily determine the appropriate size for their needs, thereby improving their shopping experience

Email

Features

Customized Size Charts

Apply size charts to specific products or entire product categories. Tailor size charts to match the unique requirements of each product type, ensuring accurate sizing guidance for your customers.

Flexible Application

Choose whether to apply size charts on individual product pages or across entire product categories. This flexibility enables you to target size information precisely where it's needed most, maximizing its effectiveness.

Improved Customer Experience

Enhance the shopping experience by providing clear and accurate size information. Customers can make informed decisions, resulting in fewer returns and increased satisfaction with their purchases.s
